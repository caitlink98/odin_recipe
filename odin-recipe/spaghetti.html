Spaghetti

Spaghetti is power

Just as Marshall's mother made, arm's spaghetto, something.

Ingredients

  • Noodle
  • sauce
  • water
  • cheese

Steps

  1. Boil water
  2. Heat sauce in seperate pan
  3. Boil noodles until al dente
  4. Strain noodles
  5. Combine with sauce, serve seperately if desired
  6. Top with cheese
