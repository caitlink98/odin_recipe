Hard Boiled Egg

Delicious hard-boiled egg

Hard-boiled eggs are a white people favorite. You can spice them up by making them delived eggs, but that is too much for some folk.

Ingredients

  • Egg
  • Water
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Boil water
  2. Boil eggs for approximately 18 minutes
  3. Ice eggs(they get their feelings hurt if you don't)
  4. Peel and enjoy
